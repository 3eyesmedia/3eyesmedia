How to setup my influencer profile?
Anybody can come on our platform , sign up, & create a profile. It’s mandatory for an Influencer to fill correct details to be eligible for a campaign and link their social media handles. Post applying for a campaign, an influencer can’t update his profile till the campaign is completed.
How to choose a campaign?
Influencers initially can start working with brands by browsing them in the explore section and applying the campaigns. The campaign should align with your style and interests, you should focus on building quality engagement on your profile and apply for as many relevant campaigns, post fulfilling the criteria mentioned in campaign description.
What are the payment methods available?
The Infloso Payout offers multiple payment methods, allowing our influencers the flexibility to complete the payment using the payment method of their choice. You can choose UPI or direct transfer to any bank account.
Do I have to connect my social media account?
Yes, connecting your social media account gives us access to your follower count which makes it possible for us to eliminate fake profiles. This also helps us in connecting influencers to the relevant brands. Don't worry, your data is safe with us.
