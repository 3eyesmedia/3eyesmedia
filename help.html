How to setup my brand profile?
Any brand can come on our platform , sign up, & create a profile. You need to select the industry relevant to your brand crucially. Further, you can link the brand’s social media profiles.
Does my brand need to have a registration?
Once you sign in, you will need to enroll your brand(s). To enroll a brand, you will need to provide the following information: Your company name that has an active registered trademark; then we require the PAN and GST details of your company. This prevents any other brand or user to sabotage your brand image by creating fake campaigns on your behalf.
Do we get a GST invoice?
If you are a verified business at Infloso, we provide GST-complaint invoices to you for the campaign transactions services. The invoice identifies the parties involved in the transaction and the description of the goods sold or services supplied.

What are the payment methods available?
The Infloso Checkout offers multiple payment methods, allowing our customer the flexibility to complete the payment using the payment method of their choice. This enhances user experience. It also allows you to utilize alternate payment methods post campaign completion, in the case of downtimes or low success rate with one of the payment methods. For example, if you are facing downtime with netbanking, the brand can instead complete the payment using either cards or UPI-wallets.
