Community Standards

We work to deliver the best user experience and recognize that there are meaningful differences between particular industries and markets. These differences are reflected in our implementation of these standards. Not adhering to these guidelines may impact Campaign visibility on the site.

Community Standards - Influencers
  • Respect privacy of other influencers, keep things discreet. If you find someone you know on the platform working on a campaign, do not disclose their details on social media.
  • Follow guidelines & terms of use to keep everything transparent, achieve optimal results with best ethics.
  • Complete your job as per timeline and make your best effort to stick to the content guidelines. If you promote the brand as good as you can, you will become a top choice for brands to collaborate with. What goes around, comes around, remember?
  • Stay active and update your social media frequently for best possible deals with brands.
  • No misleading information about yourself or false representation of someone else. Do not harass or bully anybody.
  • No fraudulent or fake reach. We detect fake reach automatically and ban accounts that violate our community standards.
  • Don’t submit manipulated or incorrect insights or reports. We manually review each promotion and it’s insights. We track the engagement through our algorithm and will know if you’ve manipulated it and it might lead to strict action against you.
  • Don’t reach out to the brand personally on any social media. Always communicate & transact through Infloso for your own security. Infloso will not be held responsible or liable if you are left unpaid by the brand or spammed. Never share your personal contact information.
  • We don’t allow defamatory, spammy, irrelevant promotional materials, indecent, offensive, profane, discriminatory, misleading, racial, sexist, unlawful or threatening dialogue.
  • We reserve the right to delete or interfere any objectionable thing at our discretion and block any repeat offenders. We will remove anyone who is fraudulent, deceptive or misleading.
  • Coordinated group attacks will not be tolerated.
  • Respect that other users in the community have had different life experiences and may have a different perspective to yours. We welcome different viewpoints.
Community Standards - Brands & Agencies
  • Ensure full transperancy, safety, privacy and respect to all influencers for best practice, optimal results, safe transactions and high community standards.
  • Follow all guidelines and provide safe, positive promotional content. Any violation of community or campaign guidelines will be strictly dealt with.
  • For best results, make appropriate campaigns with adequate budgets, which are broad enough for influencers to apply.
  • Do not have unrealistic expectations. Remember, influencers are humans and they work really hard to create content to promote your brand. If you’re nice to them, they will be nice to your brand.
  • No misleading, hidden information. People don’t like surprises in business.
  • Don’t contact any influencer personally on any social media. Always communicate and transact through infloso for your own security. Infloso will not be held responsible or liable if the influencer does not promote, deletes the post, cheats, spams, etc. Never share your personal contact information.
  • Don’t use abusive, offensive, racist, illegal, derogatory, discriminatory, obscene, harassing, bullying, propogandic content or communication. We are very solid when it comes to our community guidelines and we want to make it a safe place for everyone, including you.
  • Do not misrepresent or pretend to be someone else. Do not promote products you do not have the rights or permission for. Do not submit fake documentation for verification. Do not hamper competitor brands by creating negative cross campaigns. You cannot promote individuals if they are not linked to a service as described in our campaign guideline.
  • We don’t allow defamatory, spammy, irrelevant promotional materials, indecent, offensive, profane, discriminatory, misleading, sexist, racial, unlawful or threatening dialogue.
  • We reserve the right to delete or interfere any objectionable thing at our discretion and block any repeat offenders. We will remove anyone who is fraudulent, deceptive or misleading.
  • Coordinated group attacks will not be tolerated.
  • Respect that other users in the community have had different life experiences and may have a different perspective to yours. We welcome different viewpoints.
